10 Personalised Services In Healthcare

The thing that customers hate the most is treating everyone same. Patients don't want to feel that. They expect personalized one-on-one service that helps them get the right treatment. As everyone's body is different.So, with the new advancements, healthcare departments are offering personalization in healthcare easily. Here are 10 services that offer personalisation in healthcare.

1. Doctor on Demand Offers Personalized Telemedicine Care

Sometimes it is very costly to visit a doctor’s clinic, but their are many online symptom checker that are mostly accurate. Telemedicine care offers a call in which doctors understand about the symptoms and tell about the disease and tell the treatment about it. Patients get unique personalized one on one care in which doctors know about their medical history and even from the comfort of home..

2. Medical Center Meets Unique Needs with Concierge Service

New Jersey’s Riverview Medical Center, knows that most hospital stop over's aren’t planned. Its concierge service helps patients who don't have the needs related to health, example- delivering reading materials or booking hotel rooms for family members near the hospital. This service is free and it meets each patient’s distinctive needs with customized service and care that is very special..

3. Orlando Health Uses Data to Help New Moms

Having a baby is a big responsibility for parents. Orlando Health uses large amounts of data from patients to provide personalized communication to new mothers. Moms can choose one choice to care for the baby or to care for the family and regularly receive email to solve doubts/ question they may have. So they don't have to search on thew internet for their questions, intead they can get their particular question answered right in their inbox..

4. Your.MD Uses AI to Create Personalized Health Recommendations

User Friendly virtual assistant app Your.MD uses AI algorithms to search for medical conditions covering 1,000+ conditions. Patients can chat with the bot and get treatments regarding their health problems, it gives fully accurate responses. After identifying a patient’s potential condition, the bot can connect the user with the best doctors in their area. The personalized, data-driven care ensures all users get the help they need..

5. Talkspace Provides Personalized Online Therapy

Talkspace makes therapy available to everyone, whosoever he/she is and wherever he/she lives. Patients fill a form and then are matched with one of more than 2,000 licensed therapists suitable for him/her. They can then text, chat or call their therapist whenever they need. Talkspace therapists provide personalized and customized care for most of the mental health conditions..

6. Michigan Hospital Provides Personalized, Luxury Service

Patients at Henry Ford West Bloomfield Hospital in Michigan mostly don’t feel like they’re at a hospital. The goal of the staff is to provide personalised service and make patients feel like they are at a Luxury spa. The doctors are very kind and the hospital cafeteria uses produce grown in an on-site greenhouse, the rooms are Luxury and designed like hotel rooms and patients aren’t woken up between 9 p.m. and 5 a.m so that they get a good sleep. The hospital realizes that patients are also people and gives them care as keep them appreciated and as comfortable as possible..

7. 23andMe Uses DNA Testing to Highlight Genetic Health Risks

People often wonder if they at risk of common deadly diseases like cacer. 23andMe uses DNA testing to give customers a detailed report of their potential health risks. They just have to send a saliva sample. These reports aren’t official diagnoses, but they can help customers know what diseases they are at risk of like celiac disease,gastrointestinal disease etc..

8. Diabetes App Provides Personal Tracking and Recommendations

Diabetes drug company Novo Nordisk and digital health company Glooko together made a personalized tool for diabetes monitoring. The Cornerstone4Care app tracks the patient's blood sugar and uses advanced research to recommend them diet, exercise and treatment for diabetes. The app uses every patient’s unique data to give them accurate Recommendations for fast and better treatment..

9. Ride Health Helps Every Patient Make it to Appointments

Transport limitations are a huge reason why many patients get care or miss their scheduled appointments. Healthcare departments can partner with Ride Health to help every patient reach the doctor’s office. Patient's indivisual needs can become the biggest roadblocks so these door-to-door service helps them get over these barriers to getting healthy..

10. Carle Foundation Hospital Uses Electronic Education for Personalized Care

Illinois’ Carle Foundation Hospital is dedicated to giving personalized care.And they are making this happen by using an electronic education platform that keeps patient diagnoses, treatments, information related to diseases and notes at one place which can be easily accessed by patients. Patients can also use the system to call staff, request food get everything they need..
